Types of Scholarships for Studying Abroad


1. Merit-based Scholarships:

Merit scholarships are commonly linked with academic prowess. A strong academic record, typically reflected in your grades, is essential. However, merit scholarships also extend beyond GPAs. Certain universities acknowledge unique talents, such as athleticism or musical proficiency. While these scholarships predominantly cover tuition fees, the exact amount may vary—ranging from partial to full coverage—depending on the institution’s budget and the student's needs.


2. Need-based Scholarships:

These scholarships are tailored for students who demonstrate a significant financial need for their overseas studies. Factors assessed often include parental income, citizenship, family size, tax returns, and employment details. If the student is independent, their personal income becomes the primary factor. Eduholic emphasizes that while need-based scholarships exist separately from merit scholarships, students might qualify for both, expanding their financial assistance potential.


3. Athletic Scholarships:

To attract top-tier talents for their sports teams, many universities, especially in the U.S., offer athletic scholarships. These scholarships, while generous, demand a balance between rigorous training and academics. Often, based on their high school sports performance, promising athletes are scouted and offered these scholarships.


4. Field or Program-specific Scholarships:

These scholarships are tailored for specific courses or levels of study. Whether it's for a particular postgraduate program or an undergraduate engineering course, these scholarships are often directly awarded by the institution. Sometimes, an affiliated private donor or alumnus might fund them. In some cases, such scholarships get automatically included in the university admission offer, obviating the need for a separate application.


5. Scholarships for Minorities:

Eduholic emphasizes the importance of inclusivity in education. Hence, many institutions provide scholarships targeting minority groups, ensuring equal educational opportunities. Whether you identify as a woman, belong to a racial or ethnic minority, or are part of the LGBTQ community, numerous scholarships are designed with the intent to bridge educational disparities.


Remember, the right scholarship can significantly alleviate the financial burden of studying abroad. With Eduholic, you have a trusted partner guiding you through the myriad options available, ensuring the best fit for your aspirations.
