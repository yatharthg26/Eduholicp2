ASK YOUR QUESTIONS
1. How to get admission in abroad university?
Research universities and programs, meet admission requirements (transcripts, tests, SOP), apply online with fees, await responses. Upon acceptance, pay deposits, apply for a student visa, and prepare for departure.
2. Do you offer complete solution for students?
We're your all-in-one solution for students aspiring to study abroad. From program selection to visa assistance, we simplify the entire journey for a successful education experience.
3. Which country offer PR after study getting job?
Countries like Canada, Australia, New Zealand, and Germany often offer pathways for international students to obtain PR after completing studies and securing a job. Specific eligibility criteria and processes vary, so thorough research is essential.
4. Can i get scholarship with my low cGPA?
Despite a low CGPA, you can still seek scholarships for studying abroad. Universities and groups assess qualities beyond grades, like leadership, activities, essays, and recommendations. Search for merit, need-based, and specialized scholarships, highlighting your strengths beyond academics. Thorough research is key.
5. Do you allow accomadation for students in abroad?
Certainly, we assist students in locating suitable accommodations near their universities, ensuring a comfortable and convenient living environment during their academic journey.
