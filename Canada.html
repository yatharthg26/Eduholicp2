Study in Canada

Canada has increasingly become a top destination for international students. As the world's 10th largest economy, it boasts an excellent educational system alongside its breathtaking landscapes, ranging from the majestic Rocky Mountains to picturesque coastlines and rich historical landmarks. With one of the globe's most significant immigrant communities, Canada is gearing up to welcome over a million new immigrants soon. Annually, its esteemed universities attract over 500,000 foreign students, with Indians leading the pack, as evidence d by the 210,000 study permits granted to them in 2021 alone. Besides the world-class education, the affordability of Canadian universities stands out among English-speaking countries, and a degree from Canada paves the way for numerous job opportunities.
